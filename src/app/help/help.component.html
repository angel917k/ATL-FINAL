Help

My tips arent submitting?

Make sure you are logged in from the login page. There will be a warning on the page if you are on the tip screen without being logged in.
Also, the tip will not submit if you do not click the agreement, or if you try to submit an empty tip.

How do I view the tips assigned to me?

Simply go to the login screen and make sure you are logged in. If you are logged in, simply click the button and enter your viewing password. The tips assigned to you will display on the screen.

Why do I need two password?

The first is to log into your account. In order to attach all the tips to your account, you will stay logged in. The second password allows you to be logged in and yet will not allow anyone to view all the tips.
